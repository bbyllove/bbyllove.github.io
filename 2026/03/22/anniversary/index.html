First Anniversary

Today marks our one-year anniversary. Even though we’ve officially been apart for almost two months, I still miss you every single day. I desperately want to tell you: ‘Let’s try again!’ But I know I can only shout this into the void of the internet; you’ll never see it anyway.

At this moment, I wonder, do you miss me? Do you realize what day it is? I hope you still recall the sweet memories we shared.

It just popped into my mind: last summer when you asked me to come up with a new nickname for you? Well, I’ve finally got one: ‘山君’. If you don’t know what it means, just google it and you’ll understand! ‘於菟’ is another good choice!

Do you think I(you) will ever meet someone else who grabs my(your) hand during those few precious seconds at a red light while driving? You big dummy! You really played a winning hand terribly!

今天是在一起一周年的日子，虽然已经正式分开快2个月了，每天还是很想你啊！好想跟你说，我们再来一次吧！也只能在网上喊喊了，反正你又看不到。

此时此刻我在想，你现在会想我吗？你知道今天是什么日子吗？希望你还能记得我们的那些甜蜜的回忆。

突然想起来，暑假那一会儿你要我给你取一个新的外号，我现在想到了，叫你“山君”，不知道的话可以去搜一下，你会明白的！“於菟”也是个不错的选择！

你说还能遇到一个开车的时候，等红绿都想抓紧那几秒钟，互相还想要牵手的人吗？你个大笨蛋，一副好牌打得稀巴烂！
